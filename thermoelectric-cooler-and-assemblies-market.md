Multistage Module, on the other hand, consists of multiple layers of thermoelectric material, allowing for more precise temperature control and higher cooling capacities. Single Stage Modules are commonly used in smaller scale applications, while Multistage Modules are utilized in larger and more complex cooling systems.</p></p>
