<p><p>Steam trap monitors are essential devices that help maintain steam system efficiency by detecting faulty traps and preventing energy loss. There are four main types of steam traps: mechanical traps, thermodynamic traps, venturi or orifice traps, and thermostatic traps. Mechanical traps use a float or bucket mechanism to discharge condensate, while thermodynamic traps use the principle of flash steam. Venturi or orifice traps rely on differential pressure to function, and thermostatic traps operate based on temperature fluctuations. Each type caters to different applications and system requirements.</p></p>
