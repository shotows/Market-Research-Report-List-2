<p><p>Centralized IP Cameras are traditional IP cameras that require a central network video recorder (NVR) to manage and store footage. These cameras are usually used in larger installations where multiple cameras are needed. Decentralized IP Cameras, on the other hand, have built-in features such as storage and video management capabilities, eliminating the need for a separate NVR. This type of camera is typically used in smaller installations or where a simpler setup is desired. Each type of camera caters to different needs and preferences in the IP CCTV camera market.</p></p>
