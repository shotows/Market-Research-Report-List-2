<p><p>Badge printers are available in various types to suit different needs. Dye sub printers use heat to transfer dye onto cards, producing high-quality, durable badges. Retransfer printers are known for their ability to print edge-to-edge on cards, making them ideal for full-color designs. Inkjet printers are more affordable and commonly used for printing on plain paper, making them a budget-friendly option for basic badge printing needs. Each type of printer offers unique features and benefits to cater to specific badge printing requirements.</p></p>
