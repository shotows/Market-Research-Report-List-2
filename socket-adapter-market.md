<p><strong>The Socket Adapter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-pin</li><li>2-pin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Socket adapters come in two main types - 3-pin and 2-pin. The 3-pin socket adapter is designed for use with grounded electrical outlets, which are common in many countries. These sockets have three slots that accommodate two parallel pins for the live and neutral wires, and a longer pin for grounding. On the other hand, the 2-pin socket adapter is used with non-grounded electrical outlets, typically found in older buildings or countries with different electrical standards. Both types provide a convenient way to adapt different plug types to fit various socket configurations.</p></p>
