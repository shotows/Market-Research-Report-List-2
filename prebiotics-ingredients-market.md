<p><p>Prebiotics ingredients market is categorized into types such as Oligosaccharide (MOS/GOS/FOS), Inulin, Polydextrose, and Other. Oligosaccharides include Mannan oligosaccharides (MOS), Galacto-oligosaccharides (GOS), and Fructo-oligosaccharides (FOS), which are known for promoting the growth of beneficial gut bacteria. Inulin, a type of dietary fiber, also acts as a prebiotic by stimulating the growth of beneficial bacteria. Polydextrose is a soluble fiber that helps promote gut health. Other prebiotics include resistant starches, beta-glucans, and arabinogalactans with similar functions in supporting gut microbiota.</p></p>
