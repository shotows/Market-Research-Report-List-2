<p><ul><li>Central Card Issuance System</li><li>Card Instant Issuance System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Card Issuance System market can be categorized into two types - Central Card Issuance System and Card Instant Issuance System. </p><p>Central Card Issuance System involves issuing cards in bulk at a central location, usually by card manufacturers or financial institutions. This process allows for customization and personalization of cards before distributing them to customers.</p><p>On the other hand, Card Instant Issuance System enables cards to be printed and issued on-demand at locations such as bank branches or retail stores, providing customers with immediate access to their cards.</p></p>
