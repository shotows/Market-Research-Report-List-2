<p><ul><li>Conventional Oilfield Degasser</li><li>Portable Oilfield Degasser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oilfield degasser market can be classified into two main types: conventional oilfield degassers and portable oilfield degassers. Conventional oilfield degassers are typically larger in size and are stationary units that are permanently installed at oilfield sites. On the other hand, portable oilfield degassers are smaller, more compact units that can be easily transported and set up at different locations as needed. Both types of degassers play a crucial role in removing unwanted gases from drilling fluids to maintain operational efficiency and safety in oilfield operations.</p></p>
