<p><p>LED dimmers are electronic devices used to control the brightness of LED lighting fixtures. There are two main types of LED dimmers available on the market: wired LED dimmers and wireless LED dimmers. </p><p>Wired LED dimmers require a physical connection to the lighting fixture and are typically installed during the initial wiring of a building. On the other hand, wireless LED dimmers rely on radio frequency or Bluetooth technology to adjust the light output without the need for a direct physical connection. Both types offer convenient and efficient ways to control lighting levels in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
