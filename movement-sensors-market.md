<p><ul><li>Active Movement Sensors</li><li>Passive Movement Sensors</li><li>Dual Technology Movement Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active movement sensors use continuous energy to detect motion, such as ultrasonic sensors. Passive movement sensors detect changes in infrared energy emitted by objects to sense movement, like PIR sensors. Dual technology movement sensors combine both active and passive sensors to reduce false alarms and increase effectiveness. These different types of movement sensors cater to various needs and preferences, offering a wide range of options for security and automation systems in homes, offices, and other spaces.</p></p>
