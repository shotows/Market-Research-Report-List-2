<p><p>Busbar protection relays are used to protect electrical busbars in power systems. High Impedance Busbar Protection Panels are designed to protect busbars with higher impedance levels, providing accurate and reliable protection against faults. On the other hand, Low Impedance Busbar Protection Panels are suitable for busbars with lower impedance levels, offering enhanced sensitivity and faster fault detection. Both types of busbar protection panels play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and reliability of power systems by quickly detecting and isolating faults.</p></p>
