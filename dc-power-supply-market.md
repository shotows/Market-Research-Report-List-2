<p><p>DC power supplies are classified into two main types: single output and multiple output. Single output DC power supplies provide a single voltage output, while multiple output DC power supplies have two or more outputs with different voltages. Single output power supplies are commonly used in applications where only one voltage level is needed, such as in consumer electronics. Multiple output power supplies are used in more complex systems where multiple voltages are required, such as in industrial equipment or laboratory instruments.</p></p>
