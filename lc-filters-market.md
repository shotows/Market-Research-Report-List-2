<p><ul><li>Low Pass Filter</li><li>High Pass Filter</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LC Filters market consists of various types based on their frequency response characteristics. Low Pass Filters allow low-frequency signals to pass through while attenuating high-frequency signals. High Pass Filters do the opposite, letting high-frequency signals through while blocking low-frequency signals. Other types of filters include Band Pass Filters, Band Stop Filters, and All Pass Filters, each with their own unique frequency response properties. These filters are essential components in electronic circuits for signal processing and interference suppression.</p></p>
