<p><p>Dynamic Shear Rheometer is a widely used instrument for measuring the rheological properties of various materials. Two types of Dynamic Shear Rheometer are Cone Plate Rheometer and Concentric Cylinder Rheometer. Cone Plate Rheometer uses a cone-shaped spindle rotating on a flat plate to measure the viscosity and deformation behavior of materials, while Concentric Cylinder Rheometer uses two cylinders with one rotating inside the other to measure the flow behavior of fluids under shear stress. Both instruments are essential in characterizing the rheological properties of materials.</p></p>
