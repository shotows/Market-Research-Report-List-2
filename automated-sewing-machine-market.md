<p><p>Automated sewing machines can be categorized into two main types based on the number of sewing heads they have. Single head sewing machines have only one needle and sewing head, making them suitable for small-scale or custom projects. On the other hand, multi-head sewing machines have multiple sewing heads that can work simultaneously, increasing productivity and efficiency for larger-scale production. Both types offer automation and precision in sewing, but cater to different production needs based on the number of sewing heads available.</p></p>
