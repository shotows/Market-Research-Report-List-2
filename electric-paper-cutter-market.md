<p><p>Electric paper cutters can be classified into two main types: semi-automatic and automatic. The semi-automatic paper cutters require some manual input from the user, such as positioning the paper and pressing a button to start the cutting process. On the other hand, automatic paper cutters are fully automated and require no manual intervention once the paper is placed in the machine. Both types of electric paper cutters offer efficiency and precision in cutting various types of paper materials, making them essential tools for businesses and professionals in the printing and publishing industries.</p></p>
