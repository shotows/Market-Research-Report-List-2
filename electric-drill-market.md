<p><p>The electric drill market can be segmented into two main types: corded and cordless drills. Corded drills are powered by a direct electrical connection, providing consistent power output but limited mobility. On the other hand, cordless drills run on rechargeable batteries, offering greater portability and flexibility but may have limited run time. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences, with corded drills being suitable for heavy-duty tasks and cordless drills being ideal for light-duty and on-the-go applications.</p></p>
