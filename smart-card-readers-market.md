<p><strong>The Smart Card Readers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact-based Smart Card Readers</li><li>Contactless-based Smart Card Readers</li><li>Dual Interface-based Smart Card Readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact-based Smart Card Readers are devices that require physical contact between the smart card and the reader for data transfer. Contactless-based Smart Card Readers use radio frequency identification (RFID) technology for wireless communication with the smart card. Dual Interface-based Smart Card Readers can support both contact and contactless communication with the smart card. Each type of reader offers different levels of security and convenience for various applications, such as access control, payment systems, and identification.</p></p>
