<p><p>Fibrous casing market comprises regular fibrous casing and peelable fibrous casing. Regular fibrous casing is a non-edible casing that is typically removed before consumption, commonly used for salami and other dry-cured meats. Peelable fibrous casing, on the other hand, is edible and can be peeled off easily before consumption, often used for sausages and other deli meats. Both types of casing offer different benefits based on the desired end product and consumer preferences in the meat industry.</p></p>
