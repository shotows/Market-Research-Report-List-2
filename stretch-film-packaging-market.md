<p><p>Stretch film packaging market is divided into two main types - Machine Stretch Film and Manual Stretch Film. Machine Stretch Film is a type of stretch film that is mainly used with automatic stretch wrapping machines for large scale industrial packaging. On the other hand, Manual Stretch Film is used by hand for smaller scale packaging needs. Both types offer benefits such as securing and protecting products during transportation, enhancing load stability, and reducing packaging costs.</p></p>
