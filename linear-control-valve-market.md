<p><p>Linear control valves are classified into different types based on their design and function. Gate valves are used for fully open or fully closed control of fluids. Diaphragm valves are ideal for regulating flow in applications where cleanliness is crucial. Ball valves offer quick on/off control. Butterfly valves provide efficient flow regulation. Plug valves are suitable for high-pressure applications. Other types include globe valves, pinch valves, and needle valves, each offering unique features for specific control needs in different industries.</p></p>
