<p><p>ELIASA market types refer to the different ways in which Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) tests can be conducted. Single-mode ELISA involves using a single type of antibody to detect a specific antigen, making it more focused and precise. Each market type offers unique advantages depending on the specific testing needs and requirements of the user.</p></p>
