<p><p>Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS) technology is used in optical communication networks to dynamically route specific wavelengths of light. There are three main types of WSS on the market: Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS) based WSS, Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems (MEMS) based WSS, and Liquid Crystal (LC) based WSS. LCoS WSS offers high resolution and low insertion loss, MEMS WSS provides fast switching and low power consumption, while LC WSS offers cost-effective solutions. Each type caters to different requirements in terms of performance and cost.</p></p>
