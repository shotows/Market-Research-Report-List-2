<p><p>Scratch testers come in three main market types - ordinary scratch testers, linear scratch testers, and multi finger scratch testers. Ordinary scratch testers are typically used for basic scratch testing on flat surfaces. Linear scratch testers are designed for more precise and controlled scratch testing along a straight line. Multi finger scratch testers allow for testing multiple scratches simultaneously, making them ideal for evaluating coating durability and wear resistance. Each type offers different capabilities and benefits, catering to specific testing needs in various industries.</p></p>
