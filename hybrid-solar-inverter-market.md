<p><p>Hybrid solar inverters are classified into two main types based on the number of phases: single-phase and three-phase models. Single-phase inverters are suitable for residential applications or small commercial setups where the power demand is relatively low. Three-phase inverters, on the other hand, are designed for larger commercial or industrial applications that require higher power output. Both types of inverters combine solar energy with grid power to provide a reliable and efficient electricity supply.</p></p>
