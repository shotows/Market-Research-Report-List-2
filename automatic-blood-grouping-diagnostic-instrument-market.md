<p><strong>The Automatic Blood Grouping Diagnostic Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nucleic Acid Amplification Techniques</li><li>Microbiological Technique</li><li>Immunohematology Techniques</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic blood grouping diagnostic instruments are currently available in three main types based on the techniques used for blood typing: nucleic acid amplification techniques, microbiological techniques, and immunohematology techniques. Nucleic acid amplification techniques detect genetic markers for blood group antigens, while microbiological techniques use bacterial agglutination to determine blood type. Immunohematology techniques rely on antigen-antibody reactions to identify blood groups. These different types of instruments offer healthcare professionals various options for accurate and efficient blood grouping analysis.</p></p>
