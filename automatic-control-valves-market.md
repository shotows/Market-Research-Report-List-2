<p><p>Automatic control valves are essential components in fluid control systems, with various types catering to different applications. Angle seat piston valves offer reliable on/off control. Globe valves provide precise throttling. Butterfly valves are suitable for large flow rates. Ball valves offer quick shut-off. Pinch valves are ideal for abrasive media. Diaphragm valves are versatile, with a wide range of materials and sizes. Each type has unique features that make them suitable for specific industries and applications in the automatic control valves market.</p></p>
