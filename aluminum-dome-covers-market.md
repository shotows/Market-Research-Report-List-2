Internal Floating Roof Systems are designed to float on the surface of stored liquids inside a tank, reducing vapor emissions and preventing contamination. External Floating Roof Systems, on the other hand, provide a roof that floats on top of the liquid surface in an aboveground storage tank, providing protection from external elements. Both types of systems are essential for the safe storage and transportation of liquids in industrial settings.</p></p>
