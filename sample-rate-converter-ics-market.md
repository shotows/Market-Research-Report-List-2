<p><p>Sample rate converter ICs are essential in digital audio applications for converting the sampling rate of a digital signal. There are two main types in the market - Asynchronous Type and Synchronous Type. </p><p>Asynchronous Type converters operate independently of the incoming signal's clock, allowing for flexible and versatile applications. On the other hand, Synchronous Type converters synchronize to an external clock signal, ensuring accurate and jitter-free conversion. Both types cater to different needs and applications in the digital audio industry, offering unique advantages based on their operation.</p></p>
