<p><strong>The Crossflow Cooling Tower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Type</li><li>Closed Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crossflow cooling towers are classified into three main types: Open Type, Closed Type, and Others. </p><p>Open Type cooling towers allow direct contact between the air and the cooling water, resulting in evaporation and cooling. Closed Type cooling towers, on the other hand, keep the cooling water separate from the air, preventing contamination and evaporation. Others include hybrid cooling towers and fluid coolers, which offer unique features combining both open and closed systems. Each type has its own advantages and is used in various industries based on specific cooling requirements.</p></p>
