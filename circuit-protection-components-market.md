<p><p>Circuit protection components play a crucial role in safeguarding electronic devices from damage due to overcurrent and overvoltage events. Overcurrent protection components such as fuses and circuit breakers are designed to prevent excessive current flow in the circuit, protecting against overheating and potential fire hazards. On the other hand, overvoltage protection components like surge protectors and voltage regulators help in limiting the voltage levels within safe limits, safeguarding the circuit from voltage spikes and transients. Both types of components are essential for ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic devices.</p></p>
