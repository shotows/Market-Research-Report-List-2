<p><p>Linear lighting market consists of various types such as Static White, Tunable White, RGB, RGBW, and Others. Static White offers fixed color temperature lighting. Tunable White allows adjustment of color temperature for different ambiance. RGB provides a spectrum of colors by combining red, green, and blue LEDs. RGBW includes an additional white LED for better color mixing. Others may include specialty linear lighting with unique features like color-changing options or advanced controls. Each type caters to different lighting needs and preferences in various environments.</p></p>
