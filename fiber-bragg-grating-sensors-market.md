<p><p>Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) sensors are optical sensors that use the principle of Bragg diffraction to measure strain, temperature, pressure, and other physical quantities. They consist of a periodic modulation of the refractive index along a section of optical fiber, which produces a wavelength-specific reflection of light. FBG sensors offer advantages such as high sensitivity, immunity to electromagnetic interference, and multiplexing capabilities.</p><p>The Fiber Bragg Grating Sensors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.40% during the forecast period.
