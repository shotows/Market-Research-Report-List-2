<p><p>Information terminals can be divided into two main market types: Indoor and Outdoor terminals. Indoor terminals are typically found within enclosed spaces such as shopping malls, airports, and office buildings. They are designed to provide information and services to customers in a controlled environment. On the other hand, Outdoor terminals are placed in open-air locations like parks, street corners, and public squares. These terminals are exposed to weather elements and are often used for providing information to pedestrians and tourists.</p></p>
