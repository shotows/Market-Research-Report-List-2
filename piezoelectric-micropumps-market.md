<p><p>Piezoelectric micropumps are devices that use the piezoelectric effect to pump air or water. Piezoelectric Air Pumps are designed to pump air or gas by converting electrical energy into mechanical motion through piezoelectric crystals. On the other hand, Piezoelectric Water Pumps are used to pump water using the same principle. Both types of micropumps are compact, efficient, and ideal for applications requiring precise control and small-scale pumping operations. They find applications in medical devices, environmental monitoring, and analytical instruments.</p></p>
