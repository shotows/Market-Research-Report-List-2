<p><p>Chip Thick Film Resistors are available in two market types: Through Hole and Surface Mount Device (SMD). Through Hole resistors are designed to be mounted by inserting their leads into holes in the circuit board and soldering them in place. On the other hand, SMD resistors are much smaller and are mounted directly onto the surface of the circuit board. Both types offer benefits in terms of space-saving and ease of assembly, catering to different preferences and requirements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
