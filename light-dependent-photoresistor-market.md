<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Light Dependent Photoresistor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Photoresistor</li><li>Extrinsic Photoresistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light dependent photoresistors are categorized into two types: intrinsic photoresistors and extrinsic photoresistors. Intrinsic photoresistors are made from semiconductor materials like silicon or germanium and exhibit light-dependent resistance due to their intrinsic properties. Extrinsic photoresistors are made by adding impurities to intrinsic photoresistors to enhance their light-sensitivity. Both types of photoresistors play a crucial role in various applications such as light detection, automatic lighting systems, and light intensity measurement in industries like automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
