<p><p>Aircraft seat belts market can be classified into different types based on the number of attachment points. Two-point seat belts have two attachment points, typically found on older aircraft models. Three-point seat belts have three attachment points, with one point attaching to the aircraft floor. Four-point seat belts have four attachment points, providing additional security and stability. Other types of seat belts may include variations such as five-point harnesses or inflatable seat belts, offering different levels of safety and comfort for passengers.</p></p>
