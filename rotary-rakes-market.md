<p><ul><li>Single Rotor Rotary Rakes</li><li>Multi-Rotor Rotary Rakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single Rotor Rotary Rakes are designed with one rotor that gathers and lifts the cut crops, while Multi-Rotor Rotary Rakes have multiple rotors that work together to gather and lift the crops more efficiently. Single rotor rakes are typically smaller and more suitable for smaller farms or hilly terrain, while multi-rotor rakes are larger and more suitable for bigger farms or flat terrain. Both types of rotary rakes are used in agriculture to gather and arrange cut crops for drying and baling.</p></p>
