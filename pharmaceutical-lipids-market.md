<p><p>The pharmaceutical lipids market can be categorized into two main types: liquid lipids and solid lipids. Liquid lipids are primarily used in the formulation of oral drug delivery systems, while solid lipids are commonly employed in the development of controlled release dosage forms. Liquid lipids offer advantages such as improved solubility and bioavailability, while solid lipids are valued for their stability and sustained drug release properties. Both types of lipids play a crucial role in pharmaceutical formulations, catering to various drug delivery needs.</p></p>
