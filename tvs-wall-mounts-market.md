<p><ul><li>Full Motion</li><li>Fixed</li><li>Tilt</li><li>Fixed Easy-mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TV wall mounts market can be divided into four main types: Full motion, fixed, tilt, and fixed easy-mount. Full motion mounts allow for the TV to swivel and tilt in multiple directions, providing optimal viewing angles. Fixed mounts securely attach the TV to the wall without any movement. Tilt mounts angle the TV downward for better viewing from above or below. Fixed easy-mounts are designed for easy installation with a simple attachment system. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of consumers looking to mount their TVs.</p></p>
