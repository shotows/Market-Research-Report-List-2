<p><p>ETC systems have evolved into different market types based on technology used for toll collection. RFID-Based ETC Systems use radio frequency identification for seamless toll collection. DSRC-Based ETC Systems rely on dedicated short-range communication for efficient tolling processes. Video Analytic-Based ETC Systems use video cameras to capture and analyze license plate information. GNSS/GPS-Based ETC Systems utilize global navigation satellite systems for accurate tolling and tracking. Each market type offers unique benefits and caters to specific needs in the tolling industry.</p></p>
