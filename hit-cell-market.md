<p><ul><li>P-Type</li><li>N-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heterojunction with Intrinsic Thin-layer (HIT) cells are categorized into two main market types: P-Type and N-Type. P-Type HIT cells have a P-type semiconductor layer on the front side, while N-Type cells have an N-type layer. P-Type cells are known for their high efficiency in converting sunlight into electricity, making them popular in residential and commercial applications. On the other hand, N-Type cells are more suited for high-temperature environments and have a longer lifespan, making them ideal for utility-scale projects.</p></p>
