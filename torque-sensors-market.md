<p><p>Torque sensors can be classified into various types based on their functionalities and applications. Reaction torque sensors measure rotational forces applied on stationary objects. Rotary torque sensors measure torque on rotating shafts. Super Acoustic Wave (SAW) torque sensors utilize sound waves to measure torque. Optical torque sensors use light beams to detect torque. Other types of torque sensors include static torque sensors, dynamic torque sensors, and magnetoelastic torque sensors. Each type has specific features and applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
