<p><ul><li>Paperboard</li><li>Corrugated Board</li><li>Kraft Board</li><li>Coated Paper</li><li>Liquid Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Printed cartons are commonly classified into different types based on the material they are made from. Paperboard cartons are made from thick, rigid paper and are commonly used for packaging of lightweight items. Corrugated board cartons have a fluted layer sandwiched between two flat layers, providing extra strength and durability, commonly used for shipping and storage. Kraft board cartons are made from recycled materials and are eco-friendly. Coated paper cartons have a layer of coating for a glossy finish. Liquid board cartons are specifically designed for liquid packaging due to their leak-proof properties.</p></p>
