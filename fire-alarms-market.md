<p><ul><li>Addressable Systems</li><li>Conventional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire alarm market can be classified into two main types: addressable systems and conventional systems. Addressable systems are advanced, allowing each device to have its own address for easy identification of the exact location of the fire event. This makes them ideal for large buildings or complexes. On the other hand, conventional systems use zones to identify the general area of the fire event, making them suitable for smaller buildings or residential applications. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in the market.</p></p>
