<p><p>Metal foil current sensing resistors are available in two main market types: Through Hole and Surface Mount Device (SMD). Through Hole resistors have leads that are inserted into holes on the circuit board and soldered in place. They are often used in applications where high power dissipation is required. On the other hand, SMD resistors are soldered directly onto the surface of the circuit board, making them ideal for compact and lightweight designs. Both types offer precise and reliable current sensing capabilities for a variety of electronic applications.</p></p>
