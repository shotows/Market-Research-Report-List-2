<p><ul><li>Concentric Reducer</li><li>Eccentric Reducer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ducting reducers are used in air duct systems to connect different size ducts or equipment. Concentric reducers have a symmetrical design where the center lines of the two ducts or equipment being connected align. Eccentric reducers, on the other hand, have an off-center design where the center lines do not align. Concentric reducers are commonly used when maintaining a consistent flow in the duct system is important, while eccentric reducers are used when changing the elevation of the ductwork is required.</p></p>
