<p><p>Hazardous location switches are categorized into different types based on their functionalities and applications. Explosion-proof switches are designed to prevent sparks or arcs that could ignite flammable gases or dust in hazardous environments. Other types of hazardous location switches include pull cord switches, foot switches, and key switches, each serving specific purposes in maintaining safety in potentially explosive areas.</p></p>
