<p><p>Plate and Frame Heat Exchangers are categorized into three main types based on their construction - Gasketed Heat Exchangers, Brazed Heat Exchangers, and Welded Heat Exchangers. Gasketed Heat Exchangers have replaceable gaskets which allow for easy maintenance and cleaning. Brazed Heat Exchangers have no gaskets and are suitable for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Welded Heat Exchangers are welded together and are ideal for corrosive fluids or high-pressure environments. Each type has its own advantages and is used in various industries based on specific requirements.</p></p>
