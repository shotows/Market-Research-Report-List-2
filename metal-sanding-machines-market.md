<p><p>Metal sanding machines can be classified into three main types based on their operation: manual, semi-automatic, and fully-automatic. Manual machines require the operator to manually control the sanding process, making them suitable for smaller scale operations. Semi-automatic machines have some automated features but still require manual intervention at certain points. Fully-automatic machines are equipped with advanced automation technology, eliminating the need for constant operator monitoring. Each type of machine caters to different levels of production demand and complexity of sanding tasks.</p></p>
