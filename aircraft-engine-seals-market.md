<p><p>Aircraft Engine Seals market consists of three main types: Polymer Seals, Metal Seals, and Composite Seals. Polymer seals are made from synthetic materials and are commonly used in aircraft engines due to their flexibility and resistance to heat and chemicals. Metal seals, on the other hand, are more durable and can withstand high temperatures and pressures. Composite seals combine the characteristics of both polymer and metal seals, offering a unique blend of flexibility and strength for superior performance in aircraft engines.</p></p>
