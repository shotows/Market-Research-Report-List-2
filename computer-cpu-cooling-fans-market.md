<p><p>Axial Fans are the most common type of cooling fan used in computers. They operate by drawing air in parallel to the fan's axis and are generally more affordable and quieter than Centrifugal Fans. Centrifugal Fans operate by drawing air into the center of the fan and expelling it at a perpendicular angle. They are typically more powerful and efficient than Axial Fans, making them ideal for high-performance systems. Both types of cooling fans are essential for maintaining optimal temperatures and ensuring the longevity of computer CPUs.</p></p>
