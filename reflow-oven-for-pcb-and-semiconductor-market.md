<p><p>Reflow ovens are crucial equipment in the PCB and semiconductor industry for soldering components onto circuit boards. Convection reflow ovens use hot air to heat the PCB evenly, ensuring proper soldering. Vapour phase reflow ovens utilize a kind of boiling liquid to transfer heat, offering more precise temperature control and reducing the risk of overheating delicate components. Both types of reflow ovens are essential for achieving high-quality soldering results in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
