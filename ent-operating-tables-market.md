<p><p>ENT operating tables come in three primary market types: electric, hydraulic, and manual. Electric operating tables offer smooth and precise adjustments with the touch of a button, providing convenience and efficiency during surgeries. Hydraulic operating tables utilize fluid power to adjust height and positioning, offering stability and control. Manual operating tables are operated manually through hand cranks or levers, providing a cost-effective option for basic procedures. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of healthcare professionals in the ENT field.</p></p>
