<p><strong>The Micro Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LR (Alkaline)</li><li>SR (Silver Oxide)</li><li>CR (Lithium)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro batteries are commonly classified into different types such as LR (Alkaline), SR (Silver Oxide), CR (Lithium), and Others. LR batteries are typically used in low-drain devices like remote controls. SR batteries are known for their high energy density and are often used in watches and cameras. CR batteries are widely used in high-drain devices such as medical devices and calculators. The "Others" category includes specialty micro batteries designed for specific applications like hearing aids and key fobs. Each type of micro battery serves a unique purpose based on its chemistry and performance characteristics.</p></p>
