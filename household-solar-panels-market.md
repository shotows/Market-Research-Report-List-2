<p><p>Household Solar Panels Market includes two main types: Monocrystalline Solar Panels and Polycrystalline Solar Panels. Monocrystalline panels are made from single-crystal silicon, offering higher efficiency and sleek design. On the other hand, Polycrystalline panels are made from multiple silicon fragments, providing a more cost-effective option. Both types of panels are popular choices for residential solar installations, with Monocrystalline panels being more expensive but more efficient, while Polycrystalline panels are budget-friendly but slightly less efficient.</p></p>
